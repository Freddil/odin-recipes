Chicken Curry

Description

A fragrent recipe passed down from grandmother to daughter. This curry is a rollercoaster for your senses creating an unforgetable aroma and leaving a hint of India on your tongue.

Be sure to use the freshest ingredients for the best results. We used farm reared, free range chicken and spices sold at our local market.

Ingredients (Feeds 3 - 4 adults)

  • 1 Onion
  • 6 - 8 Chicken peices
  • 2 - 3 Large potatoes
  • 2 sticks of Cinnamon
  • 15g Curry powder
  • 3 - 4 Curry leaves
  • 2 teaspoons Tumeric
  • 3 - 4 Star anise
  • Chopped garlic
  • Grated ginger
  • 15g Coriander

Steps

  1. Clean chicken, chop onion, potatoes, garlic and ginger
  2. Saute onion on medium heat until brown
  3. Add garlic, ginger and spice with 50ml water and simmer until a paste
  4. Add chicken ensuring you coat all surface area, leave to cook for 5 mins until brown
  5. Add water until chicken is submerged. Cook for 45 mins with lid on ensuring a slow simmer
  6. Add Potatoes and allow to cook for another 15 minutes. Do not overcook!
  7. Add coriander before serving
  8. Optional: Serve with tomoto salsa side dish
